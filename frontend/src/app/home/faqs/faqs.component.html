What is GMS?

GMS is an all-in-one excellent digital platform for automobile garages that helps them to manage and reduce the administration work & time and helps to grow their business. GMS built by automobile domain experts to keep track of customers who visits the garage.

Is my data safe?

Yes, GMS adopts high encryption and physical security measures to guard against unauthorized access to data. For more information please read our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.

Will updates be available to me? Will you charge extra for the updates?

Yes, and all the updates will be available to you free of cost.

Is a monthly pass plan available?

No, currently we dont have monthly pass plans.

Can I get part of my car?

Yes, We have all branded parts for all cars.
